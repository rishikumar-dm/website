Our Process

How We Turn an Invisible Practice
into a Patient-Generating Machine

Every practice we work with goes through the same four-step playbook. It's not complicated. But it demands precision, patience, and content that actually answers what patients are searching for at 2 AM.

01
🔬

Market Research & Gap Analysis

Before writing a single word, we map your entire competitive landscape. We identify the keywords your competitors aren't covering, the questions patients are asking, and the exact content gaps that are costing you new patients every single day.

02
📝

Cornerstone Content Strategy

We don't write 500-word filler pages. We build 1,500–2,500-word cornerstone guides for every condition, treatment, and symptom you treat — optimized for voice search, featured snippets, and local intent so patients find you, not your competitor.

03
📍

Google Business Profile Domination

We update your Google Business Profile twice every week with patient questions, service posts, and geo-targeted content. Even with fewer reviews than competitors, our clients consistently rank above them because of content relevancy and posting frequency.

04
🔗

Strategic Link Building

We repurpose your content across Quora, Medium, LinkedIn, and Pinterest — building 1–2 quality backlinks per month from legitimate platforms. No Fiverr links. No HARO spam. No AI-generated filler. Just real authority signals that compound over time.

Why It Works

We Don't Focus on Title Tags.
We Focus on What Patients Want.

Most agencies obsess over meta descriptions and keyword density. We obsess over the question a patient is typing into Google at 2 AM. That single shift in philosophy is why our clients' pages rank nationally while their competitors' sites stay invisible.

  • We find the exact question patients are searching — then answer it completely, with home remedies, medications, symptoms, FAQs, and when to see a doctor.
  • We optimize every page for voice search so patients asking "best cardiologist near me" find your practice first.
  • We build location-specific content so you rank for patients in your city, not nationwide visitors who'll never show up.
  • We never use AI-generated filler. We tested 1,000 AI articles — nearly all got penalized by Google. Every word we publish is written by human medical writers.
  • We focus on conversion: every page has a visible "Call Now" button because the goal is patient calls, not website visitors.
