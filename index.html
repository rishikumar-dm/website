Stop Making These Expensive Mistakes

If you've been working with a marketing agency and not seeing results, chances are one or more of these is happening to your practice right now.

1 Hiring Cheap SEO That Hurts Your Rankings

Low-cost SEO people don't help you — they hurt you. They create duplicate content, thin pages, category pages, and tag pages full of nonsense. They use AI to write content that provides zero value to your patients. Google sees this and pushes you down instead of up.

2 Websites That Take 10 Seconds to Load

Cheap developers build websites covered in iframes that Google can't even crawl. Most of these sites take 5 to 10 seconds to load on mobile. 80 to 90 percent of your patients are searching on their phones. If your site doesn't load in 3 seconds, they leave and book with your competitor.

3 Paying for Channels Instead of Results

Most agencies sell you services — SEO package, social media package, PPC package — and charge separately for each one. They focus on delivering the service, not on whether the service is actually bringing you patients. You end up paying for three channels and none of them are producing revenue.

Our approach: We don't charge you for every channel separately. We focus on what drives revenue for your specific practice and market. One practice might need local SEO. Another might need Google Ads. Another might need a complete website overhaul. We figure out what works for YOU, not what's easiest for us to sell.
