Why medspa SEO stalls

Five expensive mistakes we repeatedly find

Most Fort Worth medspas don't need "more SEO." They need diluted pages, bad architecture and conversion leaks removed first — the heart of our medspa SEO audit.

Mistake 01

Keyword cannibalization

Botox repeated across the homepage, service pages, categories, tags and blogs — forcing Google to choose between several weak pages instead of one strong one.

Mistake 02

Geo-flooded suburb pages

Dozens of near-duplicate city pages with no local authority that make the whole site look manufactured and thin to Google.

Mistake 03

A blog where a service page belongs

A "microneedling in Fort Worth" search deserves a conversion-ready treatment page — not a 700-word generic post with no proof or booking path.

Mistake 04

No internal patient journey

Pages rank but never lead the reader to the right treatment, provider proof, pricing context or a consultation.

Mistake 05

Vanity reporting

Traffic and impressions get celebrated while calls, bookings, show rate, treatment value and collected revenue go untracked.
