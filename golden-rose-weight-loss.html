More Than Medication

Many clinics only focus on the shot. At Golden Rose, we look at the whole picture — because lasting change is rarely about one prescription.

A program that works asks more than "what medication are you on?" Before we recommend anything, we look at:

  • What you're actually eating, day to day
  • Whether your routine feels consistent or chaotic
  • If appetite is quietly making progress harder
  • How your energy is shifting
  • How your face and skin may change as weight comes off

Questions, Answered

Before you begin

What's included in the weight loss program?
Depending on the package you choose, your program may include nutrition guidance, a supplement protocol, coaching emails, a workout calendar, wellness injections, follow-up care, labs, GLP-1 medication options, IV therapy, and aesthetic support.
Do I need semaglutide to join?
No. Not every package is built around semaglutide. Many women start with the Basic Weight Wellness Package, which focuses on nutrition, supplements, injections, coaching, and accountability.
Is tirzepatide available?
Tirzepatide may be added as an option when appropriate. During your consultation, your provider will discuss eligibility, cost, and whether it makes sense for your plan.
How long is the Basic Weight Wellness Package?
It's a 12-week program that includes nutrition support, weekly coaching emails, private group access, weekly wellness and L-carnitine injections, Calocurb appetite support, and monthly follow-up.
Are labs included?
Labs are included in select packages. The Ultimate Golden package includes blood work once, and the Platinum Rose package also includes labs once.
Does the program include nutrition support?
Yes. Depending on the package, this may include an anti-inflammatory diet plan, a supplement protocol, a nutrition notebook, and coaching to help you stay consistent.
Can weight loss affect my face or skin?
It can. Some women notice changes in facial volume, skin firmness, or jawline and neck definition. Aesthetic options such as Morpheus8, Sculptra, CoolPeel, and neurotoxin can be discussed if they fit your goals.
How do I know which package is right for me?
The best fit depends on your goals, health history, interest in medication support, and how much guidance you want. Your consultation is where we review all of that and recommend the right level for you.
Where is the program available?
Golden Rose Wellness & Medical Spa serves women in Apopka, Orlando, and nearby areas of Central Florida from our Apopka location. The best first step is to schedule a consultation.
