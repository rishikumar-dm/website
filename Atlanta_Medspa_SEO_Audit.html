Why medspa SEO stalls

Five medspa SEO mistakes that stall ranking and waste medspa traffic

Every Atlanta medspa above makes at least one of these — and so do most med spas. They're the heart of our medspa SEO audit.

Mistake 01

Mistaking franchise traffic for local dominance

dermani's 24,161 visits look like #1 — until you see 55% aren't in Atlanta and it sits #56 for “med spa atlanta.” Volume from other cities books no patients here.

Mistake 02

Holding the brand term, abandoning the treatments

Radiance and Ellemes rank #1 for “med spa atlanta,” then leave botox (#8), fillers (#34) and kybella (#42) undefended. The brand term doesn't book the treatment.

Mistake 03

National blog content where an Atlanta page belongs

Dermaplaning-aftercare and Nutrafol-promo pages pull traffic that never books in Atlanta. A local treatment search deserves a conversion-ready Atlanta page, not a national explainer.

Mistake 04

Ignoring the easy, high-CPC money terms

“dermal fillers atlanta” (KD 4), “kybella atlanta” (KD 6) and “morpheus8 atlanta” (KD 5) sit unclaimed on page 2+ — trivially winnable searches worth real ad dollars.

Mistake 05

Vanity reporting

“24,161 visits” and “1,480 referring domains” get celebrated while calls, bookings, show rate, treatment value and collected revenue go untracked.
