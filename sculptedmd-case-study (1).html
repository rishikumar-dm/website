The challenge

The Texas Medspa SEO Problem: New Locations, Zero Visibility

Sculpted MD runs HRT, TRT, peptide, and medical weight loss clinics across six states. When new Texas locations opened, competitors in every city already had 50 to 500+ Google reviews and years of map-pack history. A new clinic cannot win that fight on reviews.

The one instruction from the owner: lead with HRT. Lowest cost, highest retention, patients who stay for years. So we built the entire medspa SEO program around reaching hormone patients before they ever type a city name.
The strategy

Our Medspa SEO Strategy: Rank for the Problem, Not the Postcode

A patient searching "peptides for acne" or "how to taper off TRT" is not searching a city. They are searching a problem, and there are far more of them in every state than people typing a town name. Answer that problem better than anyone on the internet and you rank nationally, with zero reviews. That authority then lifts every local Texas page underneath it.

The bet in one line: the brand name gets you patients who already know you. Content gets you everyone who doesn't. There are a hundred times more of them.
