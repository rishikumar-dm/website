Why medspa SEO stalls

Five expensive mistakes we repeatedly find

It's a Secret Med Spa makes most of these — and so do most Dallas medspas. They're the heart of our medspa SEO audit.

Mistake 01

Chasing pageviews, not patients

A peptide blog pulling 22,073 monthly visits looks like a win — until you see it earns $465 and books nobody. Traffic that can't convert is a vanity metric.

Mistake 02

Geo-flooded location pages

Near-duplicate city pages (Dallas, Scottsdale, Fort Worth…) with thin, templated content that never builds real local authority anywhere.

Mistake 03

A blog where a service page belongs

"Fillers dallas" and "non-surgical nose job dallas" deserve conversion-ready treatment pages — not a research post, which is why they don't rank for them at all.

Mistake 04

No internal patient journey

National blog readers land, read, and leave. Nothing routes them to a Dallas treatment, provider proof, pricing or a consultation.

Mistake 05

Vanity reporting

"36,600 visits" gets celebrated while calls, bookings, show rate, treatment value and collected revenue go untracked.
