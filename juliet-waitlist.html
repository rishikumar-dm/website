The Science

Built different. Proven different.

Juliet's Emotional LLM (EIM-1) was built from scratch — not a wrapper, not a chatbot with empathy bolted on. Purpose-engineered at the tokenization level for emotional intelligence.

Tone of Voice Detection

Acoustic emotion mapping detects subtle vocal cues. Pitch, pace, and intensity reveal hidden feelings beneath what's being said.

Typing Rhythm Analysis

Rapid bursts or hesitant pauses sense anxiety or agitation. Speed patterns expose emotional states that words alone cannot.

Micro-Pause Recognition

Silence speaks volumes. Brief gaps indicate hesitation, stress, or deep thought processing — signals other AI systems ignore.

Technical edge: EIM-1's emotion embedding layer operates at the tokenization level — allowing real-time emotional context to influence every aspect of response generation, not just post-processing sentiment analysis.
